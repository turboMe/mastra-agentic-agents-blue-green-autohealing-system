# The shift from traditional wholesale to agile digital procurement (EN)

The traditional wholesale model in gastronomy is undergoing a massive transformation. I see it happening everywhere.

It used to be about one supplier and a fixed schedule. Today, in an era of supply chain instability, agility is everything. Restaurateurs are no longer just looking for a "supplier"—they are looking for "availability."

When building GastroBridge, our goal isn't to be just another product catalog. We want to provide a tool that allows you to instantly check who has fresh basil or a specific meat batch available today, without making dozens of phone calls.

This isn't a battle against wholesalers; it is an evolution toward transparency.

What is your approach? Do you prefer loyalty to a single supplier or the flexibility of multiple sources?
