# Digitalization of the food supply chain (EN)

Digitalizing the food supply chain in HoReCa is no longer a trend—it is an operational necessity.

Market trends show a clear shift: restaurateurs are moving away from reliance on single, rigid suppliers in favor of platforms that provide real-time visibility into the inventory of multiple vendors simultaneously.

Why does this matter?
1. Reducing food waste through better procurement planning.
2. Stabilizing food costs through rapid price comparison.
3. Building resilience against stockouts from key partners.

GastroBridge bridges these worlds, giving restaurateurs a tool to manage procurement risk in real-time.
